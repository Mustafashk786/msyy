Poetry for Fatima

Chup rehta hoon, par dil mein ek raaz hai,
Woh har lamha meri duaon ke saath hai.
Na chhune ki chahat, na nazdeekiyon ka nasha,
Bas door se dekhna bhi mere liye ibadat jaisa.
Flowers bloom when you pass by,
A glow like stars lights up the sky.
Time slows down when you're near,
I lose myself, my world is clear.
My heart beats just for you, so true,
A dream I wish would one day come true.
